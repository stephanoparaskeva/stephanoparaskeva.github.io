A task management app similar to Trello that allows the user to create groups based on events, and tasks to-do. The user can make friends and allocate friends to the groups they own to create private parties.

about
Hey, my name's Stephano. I'm a full-stack Web-Developer & Software Engineer from London.
After developing momentum during A-Levels where I achieved A’s or higher, I joined a General Assembly course to kick-start my career in the tech industry. I made this jump after carefully considering my future and realising that no matter what subject area or fields I’ve worked in, the tasks that I found most rewarding were those that posed the greatest challenge. I love facing a situation that requires thought, coming up with a plan and taking action; but more importantly, my passion lies with the moments after I fail, when I approach the problem again having learned from my mistakes and finding a new solution. I found that coding was a microcosm of this and so have immersed myself into it.
The Web Development Immersive course has been a platform to focus all of my time to learn and practice code. Allowing me to bundle together all of the things I enjoy doing and push me further to progress my technical skills ready to begin a career as a developer.
